Our Mission

Our mission is to bring awareness to the alarming amount of pollution in the ocean and how it affects the environment.
With only 30 hours, time was short, but coffee was plentiful.


We wanted to make an interactive webpage that users could go to and get a feeling for how pollution might affect life in the ocean now, and in the future.
Data simulations and graphs do a great job of showing data, but by making a game, we can show the data in an interactive, and potentially fun manner.
