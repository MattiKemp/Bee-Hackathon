About the Data

The data used was an estimated amount of microplastics and macroplastics in the surface ocean between the years 1950 and 2050.

Accurate readings were taken up to 2019, then estimations were made beyond that for 3 possible outcomes:
-Emissions grow through 2050
-Emissions level off in 2020
-Emissions stop in 2020
